Theses

  • Concordances in Positive Scalar Curvature and Index Theory, PhD-Thesis, University of Göttingen (2022). arXiv:2303.07844

    In my PhD thesis, I contructed with the help of cubical set theory, an "approximation space" to the space of positive scalar curvature metrics whose homotopy groups are precisely concordance classes of positive scalar curvatures. I further showed that the index difference factors though that space. To achieve this, I established a new model for the classifying space of real K-theory using invertible pseudo Dirac operators.

  • Pin Bordism in Low Degrees of Classifying Spaces, Master's-Thesis, University of Göttingen (2017). Available here

    In my Master Thesis I answered the question of Edward Witten what the fourth Pin bordism group of the classifying space BO(2) is. To achieve this, I modelled this classifying space as an infinite dimensional Dold-manifold and used the Atiyah-Hirzebuch spectral sequence to calculate the first five Pin bordism group of that space.
